What is biomes?

Biomes is a visual atlas of Earth's major ecosystems. From the dense canopies of tropical rainforests to the frozen expanses of polar tundra, we document the extraordinary diversity of life across our planet. We document five major biome categories, each home to unique plant and animal life shaped by climate, geography, and evolution. We believe that understanding our planet's ecosystems is the first step toward protecting them. Our goal is to make biodiversity education accessible, engaging, and inspiring for everyone — from curious students to lifelong nature lovers.
